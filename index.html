Zhihe (Lucas) Lu

Dr. Zhihe Lu is an assistant professer at Hamad Bin Khalifa University (HBKU), working on machine learning and computer vision. He has published 18 technical papers in leading venues, including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICML, ACM MM, and top journals like IEEE TIP and PR. Before joining HBKU, he was a research fellow at National University of Singapore, working with Prof. Xinchao Wang. He finished the PhD degree at University of Surrey under the supervision of Prof. Tao Xiang and Prof. Yi-Zhe Song and the Master's degree at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Ran He and Prof. Zhaoxiang Zhang. He was also fortunate to work/collaborate with Dr. Xiatian Zhu, Prof. Timothy Hospedales, Dr. Da Li, Dr. Sen He and Prof. Shuicheng Yan.
